# Admissions Section Images

Place the following images in this folder:

## documents.jpg
- **Purpose**: Required documents card image
- **Recommended size**: 800x600px or 4:3 aspect ratio
- **Content**: Documents, forms, paperwork, or school office
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File name**: `documents.jpg`

## enrollment.jpg
- **Purpose**: Enrollment information card image
- **Recommended size**: 800x600px or 4:3 aspect ratio
- **Content**: School office, enrollment desk, welcoming environment, or calendar
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File name**: `enrollment.jpg`

These images will appear at the top of their respective cards in the Admissions section.
